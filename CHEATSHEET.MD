### ðŸ§­ SOLID Principles

**Guidelines for clean OOP design:**

1. **S**ingle Responsibility â†’ one class = one purpose
2. **O**pen/Closed â†’ open for extension, closed for modification
3. **L**iskov Substitution â†’ subclasses should stand in for parents
4. **I**nterface Segregation â†’ many small interfaces > one big interface
5. **D**ependency Inversion â†’ depend on abstractions, not concretes

Think: keep classes focused, flexible, swappable, and decoupled.
